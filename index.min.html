Privacy Policy

At Fireccin, your privacy is the most important to us.

We collect personal information from our clients in order to provide our services. This information may include:
  • Name
  • Email adress
  • Phone number
  • Payment information

We use personal information to provide our services to our clients. This may include:

  • Creating and managing social media campaigns
  • Designing and developing websites
  • Processing payments

We may also use personal information to communicate with our clients about our services and to send promotional materials.

We use non-personal information to analyze website usage and to improve our services.

We take the security of our clients' personal information seriously. We use industry-standard security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

We restrict access to personal information to authorized personnel only. We also use secure payment processing systems to protect payment information.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer personal information to third parties. We may, however, share personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in providing our services. These service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of personal information.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify our clients of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
